BrannonWorks.com

BrannonWorks started as a class project; a way to showcase a "portfolio". The class is long over but the site has become a small obsession of mine. It has evolved from a small project site to a full-blown online showcase. Since its birth it has seen the addition of a blog and started to develop a web design gallery. The blog started as a side project to experiment with Wordpress and is now the newest addition to the family. At any one time, it can show current projects, links to other very cool projects, links I want to share or even just things I don’t want to forget; it’s sort of an online database.

Spencer Canoes | Shady Grove Campground

Spencer Canoes & Shady Grove Campground is a small campground in Martindale, Texas. The Manager needed an updated site, as the old one became graphically outdated and spyware infested. I worked on a team of four to design and develop their new website. The results are a site with both the campground, and their sister site "Spencer Canoes" enveloped in one place.
